About Us

Geocelestial Ltd is a registered company in Kenya offering professional services in land surveying, Geospatial Information Systems (GIS), Remote Sensing, Environmental Impact Assessment, Environmental Audit, and Information Technology.

Founded in 2008, we have grown from a small surveying consultancy to a multidisciplinary firm trusted by government agencies, private developers, and international NGOs. Our team blends technical expertise with innovation to solve complex spatial problems.

We meet this broad spectrum of surveying and engineering needs by delivering high-quality work within reliable turnaround timelines, all while maintaining the highest levels of professionalism and integrity.

Our Core Values

  • ✅ Precision – Accuracy in every measurement.
  • ✅ Innovation – Applying modern tools and methods.
  • ✅ Integrity – Transparent and ethical practices.
  • ✅ Sustainability – Protecting the environment in all our projects.

Our Mission

To provide innovative geospatial solutions that drive sustainable development across East Africa.

Our Vision

To be the premier geospatial services provider in Africa by 2030.

Our Impact

Over the past 5 years, we have successfully executed over 120 projects, mapped more than 50,000 hectares, and provided critical environmental assessments that have guided major infrastructure developments in Kenya and beyond.

Remote Sensing Services

Our partnerships with global data collectors like NASA, USGS, Kenya Space Agency, among others; gives us access to a wide range of remotely acquired satellite data. Moreover, our team in partnership with KCAA has been licensed to operate Unmanned Aerial Vehicles/ Drones; facilitating fast massive data collection as well as in inaccessible areas.
